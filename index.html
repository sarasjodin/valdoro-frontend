Welcome to Valdoro

A place to slow down and reconnect

Valdoro offers a collection of traditional alpine baitas designed for quiet mountain stays, slower days and simple living close to nature.

Some of the cabins are located close to one another, making it easy to book several baitas together for families or groups of friends while still enjoying private spaces and separate accommodation.

All baitas have access to nearby hiking paths and day trips leading to forest trails, mountain viewpoints, natural springs and alpine lakes throughout the area.

The purpose

Valdoro is a fictional retreat and mountain escape created as part of my web development studies at Mid Sweden University.

The project focuses on creating a modern, responsive and user-friendly website inspired by nature, relaxation and countryside experiences.

Technology stack

The website uses a headless WordPress architecture where WordPress functions as a content management system while the frontend is built separately for improved flexibility and performance.
