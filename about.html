About Valdoro

A small alpine retreat with seven baitas

Valdoro is built around Baita Valdoro, the largest cabin in the center of the area. Around it, six smaller baitas are placed across nearby forest paths, lakes and mountain trails.

Some baitas are close enough to book together, which makes the area practical for families and groups of friends who want to stay near one another while still having separate accommodation.

Other baitas are more spread out, giving guests quieter stays closer to lakes, viewpoints, springs and longer walking routes.

How it works

Simple mountain stays

Stay separately

Each baita has its own private space, sleeping area and basic facilities for a quiet stay.

Meet outside

Shared walks, outdoor meals and evenings by the fire are easy to plan between nearby baitas.

Explore nearby

Trails lead from the cabins to viewpoints, springs, lakes and forest paths around the area.

Our approach

Quiet, practical and close to nature

  • Small-scale stays instead of large resort buildings
  • Walking paths between cabins and nearby nature areas
  • Simple interiors with practical comfort
  • Clear information before arrival
  • Seasonal access depending on weather and trail conditions

The purpose

Valdoro is a fictional retreat and mountain escape created as part of my web development studies at Mid Sweden University.

The project focuses on creating a modern, responsive and user-friendly website inspired by nature, relaxation and countryside experiences.

Technology stack

The website uses a headless WordPress architecture where WordPress functions as a content management system while the frontend is built separately for improved flexibility and performance.
